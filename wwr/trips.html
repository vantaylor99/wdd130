Difficulty: ★★★★★ (Advanced)
Duration: Multi-Day (2-3 Days)
Best For: Hardcore adventurers, experienced rafters

For those craving the ultimate rafting challenge, Los Chivitos is an expedition-style adventure that pushes both skill and endurance to the limit. This multi-day journey through Peru's most remote and untouched river system features Class IV-V rapids, steep drops, and intense white-water action that will test even the most experienced rafters. Navigate massive waves and tight canyon passages by day, and relax by the campfire under a starlit sky by night. With rugged terrain, extreme rapids, and the beauty of the wild Peruvian landscape, Los Chivitos is an experience you'll never forget.

Highlights:
✔ Multi-day rafting adventure deep in the wilderness
✔ Extreme Class IV-V rapids for serious thrill-seekers
✔ Remote river camping with stunning Andean scenery

Difficulty: ★★☆☆☆ (Beginner to Intermediate)
Duration: Full-Day Trip
Best For: Families, first-timers, nature lovers

Looking for an adventure that blends excitement with breathtaking scenery? La Cáceres is the perfect choice for those who want a thrilling yet manageable white-water rafting experience. This Class II-III route winds through lush rainforests, cascading waterfalls, and towering canyon walls, offering a stunning natural backdrop for your journey. Experience gentle rapids that provide just the right amount of fun, with calmer sections ideal for relaxing and spotting wildlife along the way. Whether you’re a beginner or just looking for a more laid-back adventure, La Cáceres delivers an unforgettable day on the river.

Highlights:
✔ Family-friendly rapids, perfect for all skill levels
✔ Breathtaking landscapes and hidden waterfalls
✔ Opportunities to swim and enjoy Peru’s natural beauty

Difficulty: ★★★★☆ (Intermediate to Advanced)
Duration: Half-Day Adventure
Best For: Adrenaline junkies, experienced rafters

Get ready to conquer the rapids on one of Peru's most exhilarating rafting experiences! El Chapulín is a high-energy, fast-moving stretch of river packed with technical class III-IV rapids that will keep your heart pounding from start to finish. Navigate through narrow canyons, dodge massive boulders, and ride the rolling waves as you immerse yourself in the raw beauty of the Andean wilderness. Our expert guides will lead you through thrilling drops and powerful currents, making this an unforgettable adventure for those seeking a serious white-water challenge.

Highlights:
✔ Intense, action-packed rapids
✔ Stunning canyon views and waterfalls
✔ Adrenaline-pumping drops and waves
