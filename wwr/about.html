Agua Blanca Rafting was founded by adventure-seekers who saw Peru’s roaring rivers as more than just water—they saw an untamed playground for thrill-seekers. What started as a single raft and a dream has grown into one of the region’s most trusted rafting companies. With a commitment to safety, sustainability, and adrenaline-fueled fun, our team has guided thousands through Peru's breathtaking canyons and rapids. Whether you're a seasoned paddler or a first-time rafter, we believe everyone deserves the chance to experience the wild beauty of the Andes.

History

Peru's rivers have carved a network of rapids that challenge even the most experienced rafters. Once used by the Inca Empire for travel and trade, they remain a lifeline for local communities. The Urubamba, Apurímac, and Marañón rivers are among the world’s top white-water routes, offering everything from scenic floats to heart-pounding Class V rapids. At Agua Blanca, we respect the power of these rivers, ensuring that every expedition delivers both an unforgettable adventure and a deep appreciation for the land’s rich heritage.
